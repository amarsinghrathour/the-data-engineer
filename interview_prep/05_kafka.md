# Kafka Interview Prep

Q: What is Kafka?
A:
Distributed messaging system

---

Q: Producer vs Consumer?
A:
- Producer sends data
- Consumer reads data

---

Q: What is a topic?
A:
Stream of messages

---

Q: What is offset?
A:
Position of message in topic

---

Q: What happens if consumer crashes?
A:
Resumes from last committed offset
